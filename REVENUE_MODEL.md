## 🎯 Executive Summary

Magic Trees Productions (MTP) is a creative developer, artist & researcher studio based in Barcelona, Spain. This document outlines a concrete, actionable revenue framework to ensure MTP can continue its work across:

- **AI Research & Development** (synesthesia-engine, emergent-atelier)
- **Investigative Journalism Tools** (global-crisis-2025-2050)
- **Creative Technology** (sound-to-color synthesis, generative art)
- **Open Source Ecosystem** (comet-trails, community tools)

---

## 💰 Revenue Streams — Tiered Approach

### TIER 1: Immediate (0-3 months)

#### 1. GitHub Sponsors
- **Setup**: Enable GitHub Sponsors at github.com/sponsors/magictreesproductions
- **Tiers**:
  - $5/month — Supporter (name in README)
  - $15/month — Collaborator (early access to tools)
  - $50/month — Patron (direct feedback channel + custom integrations)
  - $200/month — Studio Partner (white-label use of synesthesia-engine)
- **Target**: $500/month within 90 days

#### 2. Ko-fi / Buy Me a Coffee
- One-time donations for specific repo milestones
- Link in every README
- Goal: $100-200/month in micro-donations

#### 3. Patreon — "Magic Trees Creative Lab"
- Monthly creative AI research newsletter
- Early access to generative art outputs
- Behind-the-scenes AI experiment logs
- **Target**: 50 patrons at $10/month = $500/month

---

### TIER 2: Medium-Term (3-12 months)

#### 4. Consulting Services
**AI Integration Consulting**
- Help organizations integrate local AI assistants (based on openclaw architecture)
- Rate: $150-250/hour
- Specialties: Privacy-first AI, multi-platform deployment, AI security auditing

**Synesthesia-Engine Licensing**
- B2B licensing for music/sound visualization
- Target: Music schools, therapy centers, VR studios
- Model: $99/month SaaS or $499 one-time license

#### 5. Dual-License Model
For synesthesia-engine and emergent-atelier:
- **Free**: MIT License for personal/research use
- **Commercial**: Paid license for commercial applications
- Revenue split: 70% development, 30% research fund

#### 6. Journalism & Media Partnerships
Based on global-crisis-2025-2050 repository:
- Data licensing to investigative journalism outlets
- Research partnership with universities
- Grant applications (Knight Foundation, Open Society, Mozilla Foundation)
- **Target**: $2,000-5,000 per grant cycle

---

### TIER 3: Long-Term (12+ months)

#### 7. Magic Trees Academy
- Online courses: "Building Privacy-First AI Assistants"
- Workshop: "Sound-to-Color Synthesis with Python"
- Platforms: Gumroad, Teachable, or direct
- Price: $49-149 per course
- **Target**: $1,000-3,000/month passive income

#### 8. Generative Art NFT / Digital Products
- Output from emergent-atelier as limited edition digital art
- Synesthesia visualizations as downloadable art packs
- Platforms: Foundation, Zora, or direct Gumroad sales

#### 9. AI Theater Productions
- Live performances combining sound synthesis + AI visuals
- Corporate events and festivals in Barcelona
- Collaborations with AI artist communities
- Rate: $500-2,000 per event
